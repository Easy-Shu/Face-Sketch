Paper Summary This paper presents a face sketch synthesis framework that can generate sketches similar to those drawn by real artists. Major contributions include: (i) a Content Network to generate sketch with facial features with shape of face preserved, (ii) a patch-based method to estimate sketch style, and (iii) a component loss to encourage style consistence between key components of generated sketch and target sketch.
